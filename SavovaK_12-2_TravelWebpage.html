Some Info

Bulgaria is a country that is located at the southeast part of Europe (on the Balkan Peninsula). This is famous for its:

  • Fantastic landscapes and landmarks;
  • Tasty food (like banitsa, or even our potatoes, for instance);
  • Incredible history.

Some Interesting Facts

  1. Bulgarians are cool!
  2. Food and souvenirs are pretty cheap.

Soooo what?

Coming to Bulgaria will guarantee you a wonderful holiday, full of positive atmosphere and great memories.
