Info and... Why should you come???

Hello, art and music lovers! For a fifth year, Art&Music School in Sofia organizes a Christmas concert which will take place on December 22nd, 2017. You will see some of our academy’s extremely hard-working, create and talented performers and bands which are already looking forward to give you some Christmas vibes. Come and join us! Let we all celebrate“the most wonderful time of the year.” ;)
When: December 22nd, 2017 at 19:00
Where: Studio 5, near the National Palace of Culture in Sofia

Program:

  1. A&M Choir - “Thank God It’s Christmas”, “God Rest Ye Merry Gentlemen” (A Capella Versions)
  2. Ivana Kostova ft. A&M Choir - “Jingle Bell Rock” (Jazz Version)
  3. Aneliya Milanova and Ivon Koleva - “Chiquitita”
  4. Kostadin Nedelchev - “Silent Night” Piano Version by David Lanz
  5. Nina and Valentin Minevi - “Halleluyah”
  6. M.I.G. - "All I Want For Christmas Is You" (My Chemical Romance Version); “Please Come Home for Christmas”
  7. FiveVoices - “White Christmas” Special Version :)
  8. Petya Slavcheva - “Santa Baby”
  9. Hristiyan Trenchev - “Holly Jolly Christmas”
  10. Silvia Tsonova, Mila Nedyalkova and Kaloyan Simov - “Christmas Medley”
  11. The Steppers - Christmas Dance
